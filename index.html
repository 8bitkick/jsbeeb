JavaScript BBC B Emulator

By Matt Godbolt. Source on GitHub. Works best in Chrome or Firefox. Still very much work in progress: keep coming back for bugfixes and more features!
Handy key mappings: F0 is F10, BREAK is F12. Hit HOME for a debugger.
To load up Elite (currently the only disc available), hold SHIFT and then tap F12.
Based on Tom Walker's b-em emulator. Huge thanks to him for open sourcing his code. Big thanks too to Richard Talbot-Watkins for his help and support.
